Game Plan
1
Disrupt Turn 1

Lead Incineroar or Sneasler Fake Out on the biggest threat. This flinches one target and gives your second Pokemon a free action to Mega Evolve or attack.

2
Trap and Cripple

Mega Evolve Gengar immediately. Shadow Tag locks both opponents in. Use Disable to remove their best move. Opponent now fights at a permanent disadvantage with no escape.

3
Clean Up

Bring in Sneasler, Meowscarada, or Kingambit based on typing. Set Tailwind with Hydreigon if needed. Finish trapped targets with priority or boosted STAB.

Key Rules
⚡
Never double Fake Out lead

Incineroar and Sneasler both have Fake Out. Leading both wastes one — only one target gets flinched. Stagger them across turns for double disruption.

🎭
Protean fires once per switch-in

Plan Meowscarada's first move before switching in. Flower Trick into a Fairy-weak target, Knock Off to strip items, or Sucker Punch as priority Ghost.

⚔️
Defiant wants Intimidate

Opponent's Incineroar Intimidating Kingambit = free +2 Attack. Their best defensive tool becomes your setup. Always bring Kingambit vs Intimidate leads.

🔒
Disable after Protect

If the opponent Protects, Disable that move. They cannot Protect again for several turns and are now trapped by Shadow Tag with no defensive option.

Weaknesses to Watch
⚠️
Fighting + Psychic combo

Sneasler takes 4× from Psychic. Kingambit takes 4× from Fighting. If opponent has both, your two physical attackers are compromised. Use Hydreigon vs Psychic, Incineroar's Flare Blitz vs Fighting.

⚠️
Fairy types wall Hydreigon

Dragon moves do zero to Fairy. Always bring Kingambit against Gardevoir, Sylveon, or Primarina. Gengar's Sludge Bomb hits Fairy for 2× as backup.

⚠️
Bug moves 4× Meowscarada

Vivillon with Compound Eyes Hurricane appears on ladder. If you see Vivillon in team preview, do not lead Meowscarada. Swap in Kingambit or Gengar instead.

⚠️
Ghost types ignore Shadow Tag

Ghost-type Pokemon are immune to Shadow Tag and can switch out freely. U-turn, Volt Switch, and Flip Turn also escape. Gengar's trapping is not universal — check opponent typing before Mega Evolving.
