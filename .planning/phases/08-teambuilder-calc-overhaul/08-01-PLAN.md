# Plan 08-01: Navigation, Quick Selection, and Stat/Move Editors

## Goal
Implement the core teambuilding and damage calculation improvements identified in the plan. This includes making "Teams" visible, allowing direct roster selection in tools, and adding deep stat/move controls to the calculator.

## Proposed Changes

### 1. Navigation & Discoverability
-   Update `web/src/components/nav.tsx` to add "Teams" to the main navigation.
-   Add "Create Team" links to the Roster and Home pages.

### 2. Draft & Cheatsheet: Quick Selection
-   Modify `web/src/app/draft/page.tsx` and `web/src/app/cheatsheet/page.tsx` to allow picking 6 Pokemon directly from the Roster without requiring a saved Team.

### 3. Damage Calculator: VGC-Grade Overhaul
-   **Stat Editor:** Add EV sliders (0-252) and Nature selectors (Plus/Minus) for all stats.
-   **Move Selector:** Add "All Moves" toggle and improve move search results.
-   **UI:** Refactor `web/src/app/calc/page.tsx` into a dual-panel layout.

### 4. Teambuilder: In-Place Editing
-   Enhance `web/src/app/teams/page.tsx` and `web/src/components/teams/team-form.tsx` to allow editing species sets (moves/stats) directly.
